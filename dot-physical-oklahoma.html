Do I need to be local to use Doctors Place in Oklahoma?
No. Doctors Place is a fully nationwide service. Our physical office is in Hackensack, NJ, but we serve employers and drivers in all 50 states — including all of Oklahoma — through our 10,000+ partner clinic network. You order online; your driver visits a site near them.
How do I order a DOT physical for a driver in Oklahoma City?
Click "Order Now," select DOT Physical Exam, and enter your driver's Oklahoma City zip code. We'll assign a certified FMCSA examiner nearby and email your driver with instructions. The whole process takes under 5 minutes online.
Is the DOT drug test chain of custody FMCSA-compliant?
Yes. All our collections follow 49 CFR Part 40 protocols. Chain of custody is maintained from the Oklahoma collection site to our certified lab, with MRO review of all results before delivery.
Can I set up a random testing program for my Oklahoma fleet?
Absolutely. Our C/TPA consortium enrollment starts at $49/year per driver. We handle federal random selection rates, scheduling at Oklahoma sites, and all documentation for FMCSA audits. Sign up through the employer portal.
Do you offer non-DOT services like titer tests and TB tests in Oklahoma?
Yes — we are not DOT-only. We offer a full occupational health menu including MMR, Varicella, and Hepatitis B titer tests ($75 each), TB/PPD skin test placement and reading ($65), respirator fit testing ($60), non-DOT drug screens, and pre-employment physicals for non-CDL roles. All orderable online and available at 170+ Oklahoma collection sites.
