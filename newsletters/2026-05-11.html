Quick Tips

Prepare for Exam Day

1

Skip the coffee before your exam. Caffeine raises blood pressure acutely. Avoid coffee, energy drinks, and even tea for at least 2-3 hours before your DOT physical.
2

Most BP medications are permitted. Taking medication for hypertension does not disqualify you. In fact, controlled BP on medication is better than uncontrolled BP without it. Always disclose your meds.
3

Home readings matter. If you have white coat hypertension (BP is normal at home but spikes in a clinic), keep a 2-week log of home readings. Your Medical Examiner can take this into account.
