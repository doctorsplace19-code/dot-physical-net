Quick Tips

Owner-Op Compliance Essentials

1

You cannot run your own random testing program. FMCSA requires owner-operators to join a C/TPA consortium. Going it alone isn't just against the rules — it's one of the most-cited violations in compliance audits.
2

Your DQ file needs to travel with you. Even if you're leased to a carrier, you're responsible for maintaining your own Driver Qualification file — including a current MVR and valid medical certificate.
3

Annual inspection stickers expire. Your CMV needs a full annual inspection by a qualified inspector. Keep the inspection report in your cab for 12 months.
