Quick Tips

Navigating the Clearinghouse

1

Check your own record before job hunting. You can view your Clearinghouse record for free at clearinghouse.fmcsa.dot.gov. Know what potential employers will see before they do.
2

A refusal to test is treated the same as a positive. No-shows, adulterating a specimen, and leaving mid-collection all go into the Clearinghouse as violations.
3

Violations stay for 5 years. Or until your Return to Duty process is fully complete — whichever comes later. The RTD outcome is also reported, which helps your record.
