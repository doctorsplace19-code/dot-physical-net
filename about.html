Our Story

Built for the People Who Keep America Moving

DOT Physical was founded with a simple belief: drivers and employers deserve a DOT compliance partner that's fast, affordable, and always compliant. Too many carriers were struggling with long waits, opaque pricing, and compliance gaps that put their operating authority at risk.

As a Doctors Place company, we built on an existing foundation of clinical excellence and expanded it into the largest dedicated DOT compliance network in the country. Every location, every examiner, every test is managed to the same standard.

From the owner-operator running a single truck to the enterprise carrier managing thousands of drivers, we're built to serve them all — with the same speed, accuracy, and care.
