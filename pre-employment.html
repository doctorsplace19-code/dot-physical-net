FAQ

Common Questions

Is a pre-employment physical the same as a DOT physical?

Not necessarily. A DOT physical is specifically required for CDL drivers and follows FMCSA guidelines. A pre-employment physical is a general fitness-for-duty exam that can be customized by the employer. Both are available here.

Can the employer specify what's included?

Yes. Employers can set up custom exam protocols through our portal, specifying which components to include for different job roles.

How long does the exam take?

A standard pre-employment physical takes 30–45 minutes. Add-on tests like drug screens or lab work may add time.
