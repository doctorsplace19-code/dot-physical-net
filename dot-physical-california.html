Do I need to be local to use Doctors Place in California?
No. Doctors Place is a fully nationwide service. Our physical office is in Hackensack, NJ, but we serve employers and drivers in all 50 states — including all of California — through our 10,000+ partner clinic network. You order online; your driver visits a site near them.
How do I order a DOT physical for a driver in Los Angeles?
Click "Order Now," select DOT Physical Exam, and enter your driver's Los Angeles zip code. We'll assign a certified FMCSA examiner nearby and email your driver with instructions. The whole process takes under 5 minutes online.
Is the DOT drug test chain of custody FMCSA-compliant?
Yes. All our collections follow 49 CFR Part 40 protocols. Chain of custody is maintained from the California collection site to our certified lab, with MRO review of all results before delivery.
Can I set up a random testing program for my California fleet?
Absolutely. Our C/TPA consortium enrollment starts at $49/year per driver. We handle federal random selection rates, scheduling at California sites, and all documentation for FMCSA audits. Sign up through the employer portal.
